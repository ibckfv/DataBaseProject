### Размер после массовой вставки
```sql
SELECT 
    '0/EC4BDB10'::pg_lsn AS before_lsn,
    pg_current_wal_lsn() AS after_lsn,
    pg_wal_lsn_diff(pg_current_wal_lsn(), '0/EC4BDB10'::pg_lsn) AS bytes_generated;
```
